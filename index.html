Abuja

Abuja is a state in Nigeria. It is borded to the north by Niger, to the east by chad and Cameroon, to the south by the Gulf of Guinea of the Atlantic Ocean, and to the west by Benin. The state is a center of transportation, education, information technology, and research, and a major tourist destination for outdoor recreation. Federal Capital Territory, the state capital and fourth largest city, is home to the stake center of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church).
